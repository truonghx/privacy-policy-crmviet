Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how users can request the deletion of their data collected by CRMViet Social.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you want to delete your personal data associated with our application, please send an email to:

crmviet.social@gmail.com

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your Facebook account name
  • Your email address
  • Subject: "Facebook Data Deletion Request"

Processing Time

We will process your request within 30 days from the date of receiving your request.

Additional Information

If you are a registered user of our CRM system, you may also contact us directly within the system for faster support.

Thank you for using CRMViet Social.
